Google Gemini is TranSFlator's default AI engine. If you've just
started with the product and haven't picked one yet, Gemini is the
one running behind the scenes.

## Strengths

- **Broad language reach.** Over 100 languages, including many
  low-resource ones.
- **Solid on business copy.** Field labels, picklist values, help
  text all come back cleanly.
- **Fast.** Large batches complete in minutes, not hours.
- **Four regions.** US, EU, Asia, Australia — pick the one closest
  to your data-residency requirements.

## Weaknesses

- **Less nuance on brand voice** than Claude. If you have a
  carefully-written tone of voice, test both.
- **Occasional over-translation** of proper nouns (e.g. product
  names that should stay in English). Review custom labels
  carefully.

## When to pick it

The default answer is *always*, unless you have a specific reason
not to.
