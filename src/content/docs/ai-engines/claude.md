Anthropic Claude is the nuance engine. If you care about tone,
voice, and how the target language *sounds* to a native reader —
especially for help text and onboarding copy — Claude is usually
the right call.

## Strengths

- **Tone and voice.** Consistently more natural on longer strings.
- **Long context.** Handles batches with a lot of surrounding
  context well.
- **Good on legal and compliance language.** Where Gemini
  sometimes flattens nuance, Claude preserves it.

## Weaknesses

- **Slightly slower** than Gemini for equivalent batch sizes.
- **Fewer regions.** US and EU only — no Asia/Australia endpoint.
- **Slightly higher base cost** to the provider, though you pay
  the same per-character rate to us.

## When to pick it

- Help text you'll show to customers in the first 24 hours of
  trial.
- Custom-label strings that communicate your brand voice.
- Onboarding walkthroughs, compliance disclosures, anything
  where wording matters.
